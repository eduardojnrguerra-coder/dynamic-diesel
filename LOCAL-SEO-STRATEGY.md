# Local SEO Strategy

Dyna-Mic Diesel is physically based in Anderbolt, Boksburg. Local copy should make this clear and use broader service-area wording without implying extra branches.

## Primary Location Positioning

- Workshop based in Anderbolt, Boksburg.
- Serving truck owners and fleet operators across Boksburg, the East Rand and surrounding Gauteng areas.
- Supporting commercial vehicle operators from Boksburg, Germiston, Alberton, Kempton Park, Benoni, Brakpan, Johannesburg and surrounding areas where practical.

These should support local relevance without being duplicated into thin service-city doorway pages.

## Keyword Conflict Notes

- `/fleet-maintenance` and `/fleet-maintenance-boksburg` both discuss fleet maintenance. Keep `/fleet-maintenance-boksburg` as the high-intent SEO landing page and use `/fleet-maintenance` as an existing supporting page unless consolidation is planned later.
- `/services/diesel-engine-diagnostics` and `/diesel-diagnostics-boksburg` overlap. Use the new top-level page for primary commercial intent and keep the older page live for existing internal links and indexed URLs.
- `/services/diesel-pumps-and-injectors` and `/diesel-pumps-and-injectors-boksburg` overlap. Keep the new page as the primary landing page.
- `/mercedes-actros-engine-reconditioning-boksburg` remains the specialist Mercedes Actros page. `/engine-reconditioning-boksburg` should stay broader.

## Avoid

- Do not create thin duplicate pages for every service and city.
- Do not imply physical branches outside Anderbolt, Boksburg.
- Do not guarantee rankings.
