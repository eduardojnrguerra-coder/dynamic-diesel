## Editorial Rules

- Write for real truck owners, fleet operators and transport businesses.
- Do not give dangerous DIY repair instructions.
- Do not claim guaranteed reliability.
- Do not claim physical locations outside Anderbolt, Boksburg.
- Each article should link to one or two relevant service pages, one brand page if applicable, and one conversion page.

## Recommended Publishing Cadence

- Publish hub/category pages first.
- Publish 2 to 4 articles per month once the service and brand architecture is stable.
- Revisit internal links after every batch.
- Avoid generating near-duplicate posts for every brand, service and location combination.
