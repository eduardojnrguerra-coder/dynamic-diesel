# Internal Linking Plan

The goal is to create clear paths between Services, Truck Brands, Fleet Services, Industries, Truck Advice, About, Contact and Request Quote without cluttering the header.

## Global Navigation

Header:

- Home
- Services
- About Us
- Contact Us

Footer:

- Keep concise links to primary services and specialist brands.
- Do not add every brand or industry to the footer.

## Anchor Text Guidance

Use natural descriptive anchors:

- "truck brake repairs in Boksburg"
- "book truck servicing"
- "diesel diagnostics"
- "fleet maintenance support"
- "complimentary truck health inspection"
- "request a quote"

Avoid repetitive exact-match anchors in every section.
