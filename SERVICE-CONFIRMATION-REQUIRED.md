# Service Confirmation Required

This file lists service details that should be confirmed with Dyna-Mic Diesel before being promoted as specific customer-facing services.

## Confirmed For Publication

- Truck repairs
- Truck servicing
- Minor truck services
- Major truck services
- Routine maintenance
- Preventative maintenance
- Truck brake repairs
- Truck brake inspections
- Truck brake maintenance
- Diesel diagnostics
- Engine diagnostics
- Fault investigation
- Performance problem investigation
- Engine reconditioning
- Mercedes Actros engine reconditioning
- Diesel pumps
- Diesel injectors
- Engine dyno testing
- Fleet maintenance
- Commercial vehicle maintenance support
- Truck inspections
- Complimentary truck health inspections

## Confirm Before Publishing Specific Technical Claims

- Specific brake components serviced or replaced, such as drums, discs, linings, chambers, valves, ABS modules or air brake system parts.
- Specific diagnostic equipment brands or software used.
- Specific diesel pump or injector testing equipment, bench testing capability or calibration capability.
- Whether mobile roadside assistance is offered.
- Whether DPF cleaning is offered.
- Whether hydraulic hose replacement is offered.
- Whether transmission rebuilds are offered.
- Whether the workshop services trailers, buses, bakkies, plant equipment or non-truck diesel vehicles.
- Business hours.
- Average turnaround times or response time promises.
- Warranty terms, if any.
- Certifications, authorised dealer status, partnerships or awards.

## Wording Guardrails

- Do not claim Dyna-Mic Diesel repairs every truck brand.
- Do not claim the complimentary truck health inspection finds every fault.
- Do not claim the inspection prevents all breakdowns or guarantees reliability.
- Do not claim authorised dealer or official specialist status unless confirmed by the business owner.
